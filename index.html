About Us

Nestled in the heart of Sudan's majestic landscapes, The Oasis Hotel stands as a beacon of luxury and serenity. Our hotel is more than just a place to stay; it's a gateway to the rich tapestry of Sudanese culture and history, offering an unparalleled experience in one of the world's most captivating destinations. Founded by Ahmed Osman in 2023, The Oasis Hotel is a testament to the fusion of traditional Sudanese hospitality with modern luxury. Our establishment is deeply rooted in the local community, showcasing the vibrant customs and traditions of Sudan through our architecture, cuisine, and services.
