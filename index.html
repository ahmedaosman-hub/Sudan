Nile's Bounty

Nestled on the banks of the Blue Nile, Nile's Bounty offers an authentic taste of Sudanese cuisine. The menu features traditional dishes such as "Kisra" (a sourdough flatbread) served with "Mullah" (a spicy stew), "Ful" (spiced fava beans), and "Aseeda" (a doughy dessert). The restaurant's decor is inspired by Sudanese art, with walls adorned with colorful murals depicting scenes from Sudanese folklore.

Majlis Al Arab

Majlis Al Arab is an opulent dining experience where guests are invited to recline on plush divans under the shade of olive trees. The restaurant serves a plethora of Middle Eastern delicacies, including "Mezze" platters, "Mansaf" (lamb cooked in a sauce of fermented dried yogurt), and "Baklava" for dessert. The ambiance is elevated with traditional Arabic music and the fragrance of "Bakhoor" (incense), creating a truly Arabian atmosphere.

Spice Odyssey

Spice Odyssey is a culinary adventure that takes patrons on a journey through the ancient spice routes. This fusion restaurant blends flavors from the Middle East, Africa, and Asia to create unique dishes like "Harissa Honey Glazed Chicken", "Saffron and Lemongrass Infused Rice", and "Cardamom Pistachio Ice Cream". The decor is an eclectic mix of cultural motifs, with a stunning centerpiece of a globe made from spices.
