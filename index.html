Nile Breeze

Experience the serenity of the Nile at Nile Breeze. Our menu features contemporary Sudanese cuisine, with a focus on fresh, locally-sourced ingredients and seafood. Enjoy traditional dishes in a tranquil setting with stunning river views, complemented by live traditional music on weekends.

Mediterranean Mirage

Dive into the essence of Mediterranean and Middle Eastern flavors at Mediterranean Mirage. Enjoy a fusion of fresh seafood, grilled meats, and vibrant salads in an oasis-like ambiance. Look out for our themed nights celebrating different Mediterranean cultures.

Sahara Spice brings the bold and aromatic flavors of North Africa and the Middle East to you. Relish in spiced meats, couscous, and tagines in an exotic setting. Enhance your dining with live belly dancing and folk music on select evenings.
